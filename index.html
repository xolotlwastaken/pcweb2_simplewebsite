Beautful

Mai is an attractive individual—described as an inside-out beauty by Kaede—with waist-length black hair that has bangs hanging on the left side with a bunny-shaped barrette in the back, blue-purplish eyes and a slim and fit figure.

Tsundere

She can be considered as a type B tsundere: she isn't harsh by default, but she can be easily annoyed by Sakuta's perverted jokes and come across as somewhat snappy or violent when he does or says something lewd.

Feeds you

In the end, Mai is definitely a kind, nice girl at heart, and has a quick wit yet is immensely good-hearted. She's unwaveringly loyal and shows the full extent of her kindness as she sacrifices herself to save Sakuta from being hit by a car.
